Emergency Definition

An emergency is a sudden situation that threatens human life, property, or the environment, requiring a quick and immediate response such as fires, accidents, natural disasters, or critical health conditions.

Emergency Types

Fire Emergencies

Includes house or forest fires, requiring quick evacuation and use of fire extinguishers.

Medical Emergencies

Such as serious injuries, heart attacks, or fainting, requiring immediate ambulance response.

Natural Disasters

Like floods, earthquakes, and storms, requiring preparation with pre-planned emergency procedures.

Traffic Accidents

Require immediate reporting to police and ambulance, and ensuring the safety of the injured.
