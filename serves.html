Medical Services

Emergency Department

Function: Receiving urgent cases 24/7

Services: Accident treatment, bleeding, burns, heart attacks, fainting

Team: Emergency physicians, specialized nurses, paramedics

Location: At the hospital entrance for quick access

Outpatient Clinics

Function: Examining non-resident patients

Services:

  • Chronic disease follow-up (diabetes, hypertension)
  • Regular general check-ups
  • Specialty reviews (cardiology, ophthalmology, dentistry, orthopedics)

Team: Consultant physicians and nurses for each specialty

Inpatient Department

Function: Caring for patients who need to stay in the hospital

Services:

  • Regular medication administration
  • Monitoring vital signs (pulse, pressure, temperature)
  • Providing healthy meals

Duration: From one day to weeks depending on the condition

Surgery Department

Function: Performing various surgical operations

Types of Surgery:

  • Major: Such as heart, brain, or tumor removal
  • Minor: Such as appendix removal or skin abscess

Location: Operating rooms equipped with complete anesthesia and sterilization devices

OB/GYN & Maternity

Function: Caring for women before, during, and after pregnancy

Services:

  • Pregnancy follow-up and fetal examinations
  • Natural and cesarean delivery
  • Treatment of uterine and ovarian diseases

Team: Gynecologists, obstetricians, midwives, nurses

Pediatrics Department

Function: Diagnosing and treating children's diseases from birth to adolescence

Services:

  • Treatment of infections, fever, respiratory diseases
  • Growth and nutrition follow-up
  • Administration of periodic vaccinations

Diagnostic Services

Medical Laboratories

Function: Analyzing medical samples to diagnose diseases

Common Tests:

  • Blood, urine, and stool analysis
  • Hormone tests
  • Virus and bacteria examination

Result: Helps the doctor determine appropriate treatment

Radiology Department

Function: Imaging internal body organs

Types of Imaging:

  • X-ray: For imaging bones and lungs
  • Ultrasound: For pregnancy and internal organs
  • CT scan: Accurate sectional imaging of organs
  • MRI: Magnetic resonance imaging for brain and muscles

Electrical Examinations

Function: Measuring heart and brain activity

Devices:

  • ECG: Electrocardiogram
  • EEG: Electroencephalogram
  • EMG: Electromyography

Goal: Detecting heart or nerve disorders

Treatment & Support Services

Pharmacy

Function: Dispensing medications prescribed by the doctor

Services:

  • Preparing special medications (solutions, ointments)
  • Providing instructions on usage and dosages
  • Storing medications in appropriate conditions

Physical Therapy & Rehabilitation

Function: Helping patients regain movement and strength after operations or accidents

Services:

  • Therapeutic exercise sessions
  • Electrical stimulation devices for muscles
  • Rehabilitation after strokes or fractures

Nutrition Services

Function: Preparing healthy meals suitable for each patient's condition

Services:

  • Organizing diets for diabetic and hypertensive patients
  • Providing special meals for children or patients after operations
  • Monitoring weight and proper nutrition

Nursing Department

Function: Providing continuous care to patients

Services:

  • Administering medications on time
  • Monitoring vital signs
  • Assisting patients with personal hygiene
  • Recording condition notes for the doctor

Administrative & Social Services

Reception & Guidance

Function: Receiving patients and directing them to appropriate departments

Services:

  • Data registration
  • Appointment scheduling
  • Assisting visitors in finding clinics

Social & Psychological Services

Function: Providing psychological and social support to patients

Services:

  • Helping patients overcome anxiety and depression
  • Communicating with patients' families
  • Supporting the poor or those in need of free treatment

Insurance & Billing

Function: Organizing payment processes and dealing with insurance companies

Services:

  • Reviewing invoices
  • Confirming insurance coverage
  • Facilitating payment for patients
