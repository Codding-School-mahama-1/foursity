Our Disability Care Services

We provide comprehensive care and support for individuals with various disabilities

Mobility & Physical Therapy

Comprehensive rehabilitation services including physical therapy, mobility aids, and adaptive equipment.

Cognitive & Developmental Support

Specialized programs for individuals with cognitive disabilities, autism spectrum disorders, and developmental delays.

Communication Assistance

Augmentative and alternative communication support, speech therapy, and assistive technology for non-verbal individuals.

Vision & Hearing Support

Specialized care for visual and hearing impairments, including assessments, aids, and rehabilitation services.

Daily Living Assistance

Support with activities of daily living, adaptive techniques, and independent living skills training.

Family & Caregiver Support

Resources, training, counseling, and respite care for families and caregivers of individuals with disabilities.
