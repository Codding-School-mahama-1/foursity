Emergency Health Information

Critical information for urgent health situations

Medical Emergency

If someone has severe injury, difficulty breathing, chest pain, or unconsciousness

Go to the clinic immediately or call for help

Dehydration

Signs: Dry mouth, dizziness, little urine, sunken eyes (especially in children)

Drink clean water with oral rehydration salts

High Fever

If fever is very high (over 39°C/102°F) or lasts more than 2 days

Seek medical care, especially for children

Pregnancy Emergency

Severe pain, bleeding, prolonged labor, or concerns about the baby

Go to the health center immediately
